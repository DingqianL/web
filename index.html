Xuguang Simon Sheng

Associate Professor of Economics

American University, sheng@american.edu

Xuguang Simon Sheng is an Associate Professor of Economics at American University. His research interests include time series econometrics, monetary policy and business cycles. He has published in Journal of Econometrics, Review of Economics and Statistics, Journal of International Monetary and Finance, Journal of Accounting and Economics, and Oxford Handbook on Economic Forecasting. He has received Isaac Kerstenetzky Award from Fundação Getulio Vargas (FGV) of Brazil and Heinz König Young Scholar Award from Centre for European Economic Research (ZEW) of Germany. He has served as an associate editor for International Journal of Forecasting and Oxford Open Economics, and a co-organizer of six international conferences.
